One Moment Journal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 15, 2026

The short version: One Moment Journal stores all your videos and location data locally on your device. We do not collect personal data, run no servers, and never sell your information. The only data that leaves your device is what's necessary to process your subscription through Google Play.

Who we are

One Moment Journal is a daily video journaling app developed by an independent developer. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at osignproject@gmail.com.

Data we collect

We collect no personal information. The app requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core features:

Camera Microphone Storage / Media Location (GPS)

All video clips you record are stored exclusively on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.

Location data

When you record a video, the app optionally attaches the GPS coordinates of your location to that clip. This allows you to see where each memory was captured.

Location data is stored exclusively on your device alongside the video it belongs to. It is never uploaded to any server, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or tracking purposes.

Location access is optional and can be revoked at any time in your device settings under Apps → One Moment Journal → Permissions → Location. If permission is denied, videos are saved without location information.

Subscriptions & payments

One Moment Journal offers optional premium subscriptions. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment details.

RevenueCat receives an anonymised identifier to verify your subscription status. For their privacy practices, see revenuecat.com/privacy.

Third-party services

The app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Play — payment processing and app distribution
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and entitlement verification

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in this app.

Data retention & deletion

Since all data lives on your device, you have full control. To delete all data, uninstall the app. This permanently removes all recorded clips, location tags, and app data from your device.

To cancel your subscription, manage it through your Google Play account settings.

Children's privacy

One Moment Journal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns about your privacy? Email us at osignproject@gmail.com.
